Brief CV
Dr. Xiaodong Yue is presently a senior lecturer in the School of Computer Eng & Sci at Shanghai University. Before joining Shanghai Univ., Dr. Yue received his Ph.D. in Computer Science from Tongji Univ. in 2010, and his advisor is Prof. Duoqian Miao. After the Ph.D. study, he also did post-doctoral research at Tongji University. From Oct. 2008 to Apr. 2009, he was a research assistant in the Department of Computer Science at Hong Kong Baptist Univ., supervised by Prof. J.M. Liu. From 2011 to 2012, he was a research fellow in the Faculty of Eng & Info Tech at Univ. of Technology, Sydney (UTS), worked with Prof. L.B. Cao. The research interests of Dr. Yue lie in the theories/applications of Pattern recognition and Artificial intelligence, in which he has the special focus on Soft computing and its applications in Multimedia and Data mining.
